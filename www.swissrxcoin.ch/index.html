The Innovation: SWRX Managed Access NFT

Our Managed Access NFT adds a new layer of security by tokenizing access rights to sensitive healthcare services and data. This digital key ensures that only authorized individuals and institutions can access vital healthcare information

Key Features of the Managed Access NFT

  • Enhanced Security & Authentication: Each NFT serves as a tamper-proof digital identity, verifying the authenticity of providers, patients, and medications.

  • Real-Time Tracking & Auditability: Every access event is immutably logged on the blockchain, ensuring complete transparency and accountability.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Fully aligned with Swiss and international standards for secure, compliant healthcare operations.
